Key Takeaways

  • Know your registration window: Mark your calendar and prepare early to avoid stress.
  • Have backup options: Identify at least one alternate section for every required class.
  • Check for holds: Resolve any advising, financial, or transcript holds before registration opens.
  • Use your degree audit: Reference it as a map to confirm which classes meet your degree requirements.
  • Reach out early: Contact your advising liaison with questions or concerns before your window opens.
